About Our File Tracking System

Our Mission

Our File Tracking System is designed to streamline the approval process for government engineering departments. We aim to reduce paperwork, increase transparency, and improve efficiency in the file approval workflow.

How It Works

Our system implements a three-tier approval process:

  1. Files are first reviewed by Assistant Engineers
  2. Upon approval, they move to Deputy Engineers
  3. Finally, Executive Engineers provide the final approval

This hierarchical approach ensures thorough review and maintains the proper chain of command.

Benefits

  • Paperless workflow
  • Real-time tracking of file status
  • Secure document storage
  • Faster approval process
  • Transparent decision making
  • Reduced administrative overhead
